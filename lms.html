LMS Modules

LMS is a unified Loan management and servicing platform built on a highly scalable technical foundation, supported by robust and loosely coupled modules. The platform offers a complete API based out of the box loan management.

Use our exhaustive option for loan servicing so that no customer request is left unmet. Complete loan servicing can be done remotely using APIs as well.

  • Generate Statement of Account
  • Payment Posting
  • Levying charge and its Payment
  • Waive Charges
  • Waive interest
  • Accrual for interest, charge and penalties
  • Restructuring and rescheduling of the loans
  • Receipt basis Charges and penalties
  • Disbursement Cancellation
  • Foreclosure Simulation
  • Foreclosure of the loan
  • Part / Advance payment

Partner with your peers and build your own co-lending product. Define your % shares for each loan component and Lentra’s LMS will take care of the rest.

Lentra’s LMS comes with a comprehensive accounting module which takes all the manual work away. Below are few important benefits:

  • Daily and Periodic Accruals
  • Custom chart of accounts
  • GL entries and Ledger Management
  • Manual entries
  • Real time generation of Trial Balance, Profit Loss and Balance sheet

Lentra’s LMS gives you hierarchical Limit management at multiple levels. You can select revolving or non-revolving limit types. You can configure limits to a

  • Group (multiple customers)
  • Customer
  • Sub - limit to a customer

Manage your entire collections and recovery using a single LMS platform. All the collections triggers are available over APIs

  • Check and eMandate (ACH) management
  • Swapping of the checks and eMandate (ACH)
  • Cash collections and Queuing
  • Security check management
  • Collection and Revenue Reports

You can ensure your portfolio health and keep provisioning of the cash for all the bad debts using our configurable delinquency management module.

  • NPA Flagging and reversals of accruals
  • DPD and Ageing of the loan
  • Provisioning buckets and their automated entries
  • Writing-off loans
  • Settlement (coming soon)

Send multilingual SMSs to your customers and keep him aware about the loan lifecycle. Communication module also helps you generate customer letters like interest certificates, welcome letters in your customized white-labelled formats.

All the data stored in LMS is accessible to you via the Reporting module. You can create crosstabs and Graphical reports using the module. You get dynamic APIs created of all the reports so that you can consume the data digitally !

All the LMS configurations are available for you to configure without any dependency on Lentra. Explore the Sandbox to know more almost 500+ configuration parameters.
